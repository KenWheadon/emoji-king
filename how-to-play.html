How to Play

Objective

Match the right icon to each message! Reach 100 points to win.

Controls

1-6 Select icon reaction
MOUSE Click icon buttons

Scoring

Perfect Match +5 points
Okay Match 0 points
Wrong Match -10 points

Bonuses

  • Speed Bonus: +1-2 points for quick reactions
  • 5 Streak: 2x multiplier
  • 10 Streak: 3x multiplier
